Find out what birds have been spotted by the community

Don't know where to look for birds? You can check out what your fellow birding community have seen for some inspiration. It may even help you grow your own bird list a bit faster!

Record your own observations!

Inspired by the community? Give back by sharing your observations and helping them find new birding spots. If you don't want to do it for them, you can jump into 'invisible' or 'anonymous' mode and just use Bird Count to keep a bird list for yourself.

Keep track with your bird stats!

It's not a competition... but it is fun seeing who can see the most bird species or quantity of birds. We give you stats about your bird watching to make it easy to share with friends of challenge yourself. You'll even have access to your life list, a list of all the species you have seen, so you can start a checklist!

Share your favourite photographs

Bird watching is about more than looking to some of you... so we think it's important that you can capture and share your favourite finds with your community. Upload a photo with your observations to enjoy the beauty of birds with your peers.
